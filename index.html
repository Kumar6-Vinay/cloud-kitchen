FAQ

Questions, answered

What time is the daily order cutoff?

Orders for the next day close at 8:00 PM. The countdown on the Today's Special section always shows exactly how much time is left.

Do you deliver to my hostel or PG?

We currently deliver across Vigyan Nagar, Talwandi, Rajeev Gandhi Nagar, Mahaveer Nagar, Indra Vihar and Jawahar Nagar. Message us on WhatsApp to confirm your exact address.

Can I pause my subscription during a break?

Yes — pause anytime from your account with no penalty, and resume whenever you're back.

How do I place an order?

Add meals to your cart and tap "Order on WhatsApp" — it opens a prefilled message to our team so you can confirm in seconds.
